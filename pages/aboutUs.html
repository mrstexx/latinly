About us

Project Latinly© is the idea and realization of three High School students. This application is intended for all who need help with the Latin language. Very quickly you can come to a solution if you have concerns with the Latin grammar.The app handles all declensions of nouns and adjectives, all conjugations for active and pasive forms, and comparing adjectives in all three forms. In addition to the normal comparisons, there is a table with irregular comparasion.You can also find over 1000 popular Latin quotes with their translaton to Bosnian or English. The application is primarily intended for High School students to work at the classes of Latin, and others. You can find more about the team members below. We also want to thank our mentor professor Nebojša Uletilović, for his support in developing the project.
